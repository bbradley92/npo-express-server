Applying for a grant?

WE CAN HELP!

At NPO Grant Writing, we will perform comprehensive grant searches and develop high-quality Funding Strategies and proposal boilerplates for non-profits.

Work with a team of professionals who strive for excellence throughout every facet of the grant-seeking process. In this super-competitive realm, we maintain our three operational pillars to help your non-profit stand out amongst the crowd: Tailored Research, Persistent Follow-up, and Visual Communication.

Breakdown of Services

Grant Writing

We will craft proposals that will function as your boilerplates for submission to various funders. Instead of a one-time service unlikely to get you anywhere, win awards with our proven, results-oriented process. We go the extra mile to secure those relationships with foundations and corporations through phone calls, meeting requests, site visits, and excellent storytelling.

Develop Funding Strategy

Finding the right grants is time-consuming. Many search platforms out there charge thousands of dollars per year just to point your non-profit in the right direction. Not only will we do just that, but we’ll take you there with the implementation of your NPO-developed Funding Strategy, updated monthly via slide deck presentations of grant applications, funder feedback, reporting requirements, and so much more!

Rewriting/Editing

If you have an existing proposal draft, we can provide professional editing or a complete rewrite to ensure your proposal is flawless ahead of the submission process. We’re happy to build off and revitalize what you already have to ensure consistency in your narrative.

Consultative

We take a consultative approach to identify grant opportunities for your unique non-profit. Schedule an informational meeting with us today.

Web Development

Need a website to promote your mission? We can build your website while applying to grants for you!

Personalized Touch

NPO Grant Writing, given the enormous workload it takes on for its clients, can only work with a select few non-profits at a time. This provides a personalized experience that cultivates a deep understanding of your organization, which is essential to the quality and success of our newly shared grant writing initiative. Let’s meet face-to-face, whether over video conference or on site. The narrative we’ll shape begins with a conversation.

Flexibility

Our process has resulted in our services being contracted annually to maximize success. In essence, the longer we’re working for you, the more funders we can connect you with. However, if “NPO PRO” retainer option isn’t something you’re quite ready for, we are happy to work with you on single projects based on your individual needs.

Value-Based Writing

All writing, research, presentation decks, and relationships with grant funders will become the property of your non-profit organization, providing you with re-usable materials, strategies, and connections for future grant opportunities and marketing efforts. Whether you require a long-term partnership or a one-time project, NPO Grant Writing can help your non-profit achieve and maintain excellence, because we strive for excellence with every word.
