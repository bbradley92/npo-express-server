Our Grant Writing Process

Transparent. Strategic. Collaborative.

Our Step By Step Process

ASSESS

We will review, listen, and build your grant initiative off the solid foundation of your mission and work.

RESEARCH

Connecting your non-profit with compatible funders through rigorous prospecting and persistent follow-up.

WRITE

We draft compelling, funder-aligned proposals using your voice and program details.

REVIEW

We revise and finalize the grant collaboratively to ensure clarity and alignment. We regularly seek feedback from funders.

SUBMISSION

We submit full and complete applications. We also address rejections with funder feedback, forging success from failure.
