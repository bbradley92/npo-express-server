Our Grant Writing Process

Transparent. Strategic. Collaborative.

Our Step By Step Process

ASSESS

We review your existing grant materials and determine a price estimate for the project.

RESEARCH

We fill in any gaps with data collection and supporting materials for your proposal.

WRITE

We draft a compelling, funder-aligned proposal using your voice and program details.

REVIEW

We revise and finalize the grant collaboratively to ensure clarity and alignment.

SUBMIT

We assist with submission or provide finalized documents for your team to submit confidently.
